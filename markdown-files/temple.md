### Deseret Peak Temple Cleaning

* **Day/Time:** Stakes/Wards provide volunteers to help clean the temple on Monday evenings. Service is from 8:00 pm to 9:30 pm.
* **Apparel:** Dress in appropriate Sunday/Temple dress. Temple staff will provide white clothing for cleaning.
* **Location:** Sign in at the front desk with your recommend, change, and meet in the chapel.

### Deseret Peak Temple Grounds Service Assignment

* **Location:** Meet at the Grounds Building located in the North parking lot between the Temple and the Chapel.
* **Number of Volunteers and Age Requirements:** A minimum of 25 Volunteers each night (should be in the young men/young women program OR older.)
* **Physical Requirements:** Volunteers should be able to dig, lift, bend, sit down and kneel for an extended period.
* **Duration of assignment:** Most assignments are expected to take about 2 hours but may be longer.
* **Apparel:** Dress comfortably to work outside according to the climate of the season.
* **What to bring:**  Water bottle, hat, and kneeling pad if desired. We will provide disposable gloves and tools necessary for the task to be accomplished. Feel free to bring your own tools if you desire.
