## Stake Adult Leadership Committee Meeting

Those that should attend this meeting are the Stake Presidency, Stake Relief Society Presidency and secretary, and high councilors that are assigned to a ward.

The stake president may also invite elders quorum presidents, ward Relief Society presidents, and others to attend as needed. Meetings are held semi-annually at the Stake Center in the chapel at 8:00 PM.
