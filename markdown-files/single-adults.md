### Young Single Adults (Age 18-30)

Information about Grantsville Stake YSA activities can be found on their Facebook page (Grantsville UT YSA).

The Tooele Young Single Adult Ward meets in the building located at 196 N Pinehurst Ave in Tooele beginning at 11:00 AM.

Stake YSA Committee Meetings are on the first and third Sunday of each month at 4:00 pm at the West Stake Center High Council Room.

Information for the YSA Wards:
Tooele YSA Ward - Bishop Charles Lawrence - 11:00 AM - 196 N Pinehurst Ave, Tooele, UT
Stansbury Park YSA Ward - Bishop Scott Maxfield - 1:30 PM - 417 Benson Road, Stansbury Park, UT

### Senior Single Adults (Age 31+)

Stake Single Adult Family Home Evenings are held on the 2nd Monday of each month with other activities as announced.
